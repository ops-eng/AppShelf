SimpleLauncher Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Developer: Efthymios Tampouris

Overview

SimpleLauncher is designed to run locally on your Mac. This policy explains what data is used and how.

Data Collection

SimpleLauncher does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores settings locally on your Mac, including selected apps, layout preferences, shortcut preferences, and local purchase state used by the app interface.

This local data is not sent to the developer.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive your payment card or billing details.

Permissions

Depending on your setup, macOS may request Accessibility permission so global shortcut functionality works correctly.

Third-Party Services

No third-party analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking SDKs are included.

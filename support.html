Quick Help

  • Open Settings to add, remove, and reorder apps.
  • Use drag and drop in Manage Apps to rearrange items.
  • If global shortcut doesn’t work, check macOS Accessibility settings.
  • Use Restore Purchases in the Pro tab after reinstalling or changing Mac.

Contact Support

For bugs, feature requests, or support questions, use your project support channel:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/ops-eng/SimpleLaunchpad/issues
  • Repository: https://github.com/ops-eng/SimpleLaunchpad

When Contacting Support

Please include:

  • macOS version
  • SimpleLaunchpad version/build
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recording (if possible)

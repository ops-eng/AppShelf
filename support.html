Quick Troubleshooting

  1. Open Settings and check apps in Manage Apps.
  2. If shortcut activation fails, verify Accessibility permission.
  3. If Pro appears locked after reinstall, use Restore Purchases.
  4. Restart AppShelf after major setting changes if behavior seems stale.
